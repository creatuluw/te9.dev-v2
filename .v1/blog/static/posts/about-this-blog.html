What You'll Find Here

This isn't just another blog. It's a playground, garden, and archive — everything in one place.

Here's the breakdown:

  • Dev Playground — Experiments, prototypes, and side projects. A sandbox where I try new technologies without constraints. Sometimes things break. That's kind of the point.
  • Notes & Articles — Personal notes on topics I'm exploring. Technical deep-dives, random interests, lessons learned. The stuff I don't want to forget.
  • Sharing & Resources — Tools, thoughts, and resources I'm sharing with the world. If something helped me, maybe it'll help you too.

What I Believe In

A few principles guide everything I do here. I think they matter:

User Experience Matters — Adoption is tied to UX. Period. Without clarity and usability, even the best tools lose effectiveness. I've seen it happen too many times.

Data-Driven Design — Design decisions should stem from understanding the data's logic. Gut feelings are nice, but data doesn't lie.

Obsess over Simplicity — Complexity might be unavoidable, but we should always steer toward clarity. Simple is hard, but worth it.

Fostering Curiosity — Recognizing blind spots unlocks new insights. Stay curious. Stay humble.

Why a "Digital Garden"?

Good question. Unlike traditional blogs with their linear, date-ordered posts, a digital garden grows organically.

Ideas get planted. They get nurtured. Sometimes they get pruned.

Some posts here are seedlings — rough thoughts still in progress. Others are evergreen — polished pieces that stand the test of time. It's all part of the process.

This space is designed to grow. Built to share.

Growing organically, one post at a time.
